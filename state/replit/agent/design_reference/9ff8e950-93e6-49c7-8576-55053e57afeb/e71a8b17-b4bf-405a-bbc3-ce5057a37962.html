About PhilASIN

Philippine Association of Salt Industry Networks, Inc. serves as the digital hub for the Philippine salt industry, facilitating collaboration and advancing the sector through innovation.

Industry Collaboration

Bringing together stakeholders across the salt industry to foster partnerships and knowledge sharing.

Innovation & Research

Promoting technological advancement and sustainable practices in salt production and processing.

Community Building

Creating a strong network of professionals dedicated to the growth of the Philippine salt industry.

Our Mission

To unite and advance the Philippine salt industry through digital innovation, sustainable practices, and collaborative networks that benefit all stakeholders from producers to consumers.

Sustainable Development

Promoting environmentally responsible salt production methods.

Quality Standards

Establishing and maintaining high-quality standards across the industry.

Market Development

Expanding market opportunities for Philippine salt products.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming the leading network for the Philippine salt industry.

Foundation

2018

PhilASIN was established to address the fragmented nature of the Philippine salt industry and create a unified platform for stakeholders.

First Digital Platform

2020

Launched our first digital platform to connect salt producers, researchers, and industry professionals across the Philippines.

Industry Recognition

2022

Received recognition from the Department of Agriculture as a key partner in advancing the Philippine salt industry.

Present Day

2024

Today, PhilASIN continues to lead digital transformation in the salt industry, connecting over 500+ industry professionals nationwide.
