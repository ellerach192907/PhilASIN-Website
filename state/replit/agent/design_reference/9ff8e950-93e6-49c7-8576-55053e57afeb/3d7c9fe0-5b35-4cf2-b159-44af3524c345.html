About PhilASIN

The Philippine Association of Salt Industry Networks, Inc. is the premier organization representing the salt industry in the Philippines.

Our Mission

To unite, strengthen, and advance the Philippine salt industry through collaborative networks, sustainable practices, and innovative solutions that benefit all stakeholders from producers to consumers.

We serve as the central hub connecting salt farmers, processors, researchers, and industry partners to create a thriving, sustainable salt industry ecosystem in the Philippines.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to industry leadership - the journey of PhilASIN.

2010 - Foundation

Year 1

PhilASIN was established by visionary salt industry leaders who recognized the need for a unified voice. Starting with just 50 founding members, we set out to transform the fragmented salt industry.

2015 - Expansion

Year 5

Our membership grew to over 200 members across 10 provinces. We launched our first capacity-building programs and established partnerships with government agencies and research institutions.

2020 - Innovation

Year 10

We embraced digital transformation, launching our comprehensive web platform to better serve our members. Our focus shifted to sustainable practices and modern salt production technologies.

2024 - Present

Today

With over 500 members across 15+ provinces, PhilASIN continues to lead the industry forward. We're pioneering sustainable salt production and expanding market opportunities for Philippine salt globally.
